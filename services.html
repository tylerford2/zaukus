Auto Detailing, Paint Correction & Ceramic Coating

Zaukus Mobile Detailing offers three core services for La Vergne and Middle Tennessee drivers: professional auto detailing (interior and exterior), precision paint correction to remove swirls and imperfections, and long-lasting ceramic coating for hydrophobic protection, gloss, and UV defense. Every appointment arrives with our own water supply, pressure washer, independent generator power, and an air compressor with pneumatic tools, so the same controlled process plays out no matter where your vehicle is parked.

Professional Detailing

Every wash uses a highly lubricated, pH-neutral, surface-safe decontamination procedure built around polymer encapsulation — physically surrounding and lifting dirt away from the paint rather than dragging it across the surface — which lifts dirt away without scratching or marring soft clear coats, paired with total mechanical decontamination inside the cabin. Drying is handled with pneumatic air tools for a controlled finish. Organized into Interior Detail, Exterior Wash & Protection, and Full Detail packages for every vehicle size from coupes to van and fleet.

Paint Correction

A permanent mechanical refinement process — specialized diminishing abrasives paired with dual-action machine polishers safely shave down swirls and light scratches in the clear coat, permanently leveling the surface for true depth and optical clarity. We also offer touch-up paint for smaller chips and isolated defects that don't need full correction.
