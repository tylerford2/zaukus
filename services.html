Professional Detailing, Paint Correction & Ceramic Coating

Zaukus Mobile Detailing offers three core services for La Vergne and Middle Tennessee drivers: professional interior and exterior detailing, precision paint correction to remove swirls and imperfections, and long-lasting ceramic coating for hydrophobic protection, gloss, and UV defense.

Professional Detailing

Every wash uses a highly lubricated, pH-neutral, surface-safe decontamination procedure that lifts dirt away without scratching or marring soft clear coats, paired with total mechanical decontamination inside the cabin. Organized into Interior Detail, Exterior Wash & Protection, and Full Detail packages for every vehicle size from coupes to van and fleet.

Learn More →

Paint Correction

A permanent mechanical refinement process — specialized diminishing abrasives paired with dual-action machine polishers safely shave down swirls and light scratches in the clear coat, permanently leveling the surface for true depth and optical clarity.
