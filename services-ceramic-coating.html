Ceramic Coating for Franklin, Brentwood & La Vergne Drivers

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds to your clear coat, creating a hard, glass-like layer with intense gloss and hydrophobic water behavior. Zaukus installs these coatings for drivers across Franklin, Brentwood, La Vergne, and Middle Tennessee.

True paint protection isn't a wipe-on wax — it's a chemical bond formed at the molecular level. Here is what actually happens to your clear coat with each of our professional coating systems.

Zaukus Signature Ceramic System

Our 3-Year Professional Ceramic Coating utilizes an advanced Silicon Dioxide (SiO2) liquid crystal matrix that chemically cross-links deep into the microscopic pores of your clear coat. As it cures, it forms a perfectly flat, glass-like top layer that dramatically reduces surface tension. The result is extreme hydrophobic behavior — water beads and races off on contact — paired with intense gloss and a barrier that blocks UV rays and chemical fallout from etching into your factory paint.

Zaukus Next-Gen Carbon-Infused Protection

Our 6-Year Premium Carbon-Matrix Coating is the cutting edge of surface management, moving beyond traditional ceramics with a cross-linked Carbon-Silicon matrix. This structure creates a far slicker, ultra-low-friction surface that prevents dirt and road grime from adhering, enhances the coating's self-cleaning properties, and delivers vastly superior resistance to hard water spotting and chemical etching for up to six years.

Precision Fusing Requires Precision Preparation

True chemical bonding is impossible over embedded road grime, iron contamination, or swirling. Because our professional coating systems require a meticulous multi-step decontamination wash, physical clay bar purification, and intensive machine paint correction to properly flatten and level the clear coat, all coating packages are handled strictly via consultation and custom quoting.
