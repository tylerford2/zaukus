Ceramic Coating for Drivers Across Middle Tennessee

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds to your clear coat, creating a hard, glass-like layer with intense gloss and hydrophobic water behavior. Zaukus installs these coatings for drivers throughout Middle Tennessee.

True paint protection isn't a wipe-on wax — it's a chemical bond formed at the molecular level. Every tier we offer is built on the same foundation: a durable ceramic layer that bonds directly to the clear coat for lasting resistance to chemical etching, self-cleaning slickness, and mirror-like optical depth. What separates each tier is the level of protection and durability we dial in. Here is what actually happens to your clear coat with each of our professional coating systems.

3-Year Ceramic Protection

Our entry-level professional coating applies a precisely metered ceramic layer, chemically cross-linking into the microscopic pores of your clear coat to form a smooth, hydrophobic barrier. Expect excellent gloss, deep reflection, tight water beading, and easier ongoing maintenance, rated for up to three years.

6-Year Premium Ceramic Package

Our mid-tier system builds a second reinforcing layer into the ceramic coating for stronger resistance to chemical etching, environmental fallout, and road grime. It roughly doubles the durability of our entry-level coating and adds enhanced slickness and better heat dissipation, rated for up to six years.

Advanced Ceramic Coating

Our flagship system applies the full ceramic build at its highest concentration for the thickest, slickest finish we offer and superior resistance to chemical etching and water spotting. With proper care and maintenance, it has the potential to last the lifetime of the vehicle.

Precision Fusing Requires Precision Preparation

True chemical bonding is impossible over embedded road grime, iron contamination, or swirling. Because our professional coating systems require a meticulous multi-step wash, thorough paint decontamination, and intensive machine paint correction to properly flatten and level the clear coat, all coating packages are handled strictly via consultation and custom quoting. Select "Book a Consultation" in our online lead form to get started.

Curing Time & Aftercare

Your vehicle is safe to drive in dry conditions the same day, but the coating keeps chemically hardening for the first 24 hours, so keep it out of rain if you can. Avoid washing with any soap or chemicals for 7-10 days — if bugs or bird droppings land on the paint, rinse them off with water only. After that, stick to hand-washing with a pH-neutral shampoo and a soft microfiber mitt, and skip automatic brush car washes, which can scratch and wear down the coating over time.
