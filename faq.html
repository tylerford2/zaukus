FAQ

Services & how mobile detailing works

What services do you offer?

Zaukus Mobile Detailing offers Interior Detail, Exterior Wash & Protection, and Full Detail at fixed starting prices, along with Paint Correction, Ceramic Coating, and Touch-Up Paint, each handled by consultation and custom quote. Maintenance subscription options are planned for approved recurring customers.

  • Interior Detail
  • Exterior Wash & Protection
  • Full Detail
  • Paint Correction — Quote Required
  • Ceramic Coating — Quote Required
  • Touch-Up Paint — Quote Required

Some services are fixed-price starting services, while correction, ceramic coating, touch-up paint, severe-condition vehicles, and custom work require review before final pricing.

How does mobile detailing work?

We bring our equipment directly to your home, workplace, or an approved location. Zaukus Mobile Detailing supplies its own water and electric equipment, so you don't need to provide either — though letting us know if backup water or power is available helps us plan longer or weather-sensitive appointments. Before confirming your appointment, we review your selected service, vehicle condition, photos, location, weather, and any add-ons that may be needed.

Do I need to provide water or power?

No. We bring our own water and electric equipment to every appointment. During booking we may ask whether water or power is available as a backup option — this helps us plan, especially for longer services or weather-sensitive work. If safe, approved access is available, final pricing may reflect the reduced setup requirement when appropriate.

Are appointments automatically confirmed?

No. Submitting a booking request does not automatically confirm your appointment. We review vehicle size, condition, uploaded photos, selected service, location, weather, timing, add-ons, and whether the correct service was selected before confirming. If we need more information, we'll reach out before confirming.

Why do you ask for photos?

Photos let us assess your vehicle's condition before confirming your appointment — things like pet hair, stains, heavy dirt, sand or mud, bugs, tar, sap, water spots, scratches, paint condition, and access or parking concerns. This helps us recommend the right service, avoid surprises, and set accurate expectations before we arrive.

FAQ

Paint correction, ceramic coating & touch-up paint

Do you offer ceramic coating?

Yes — by consultation and custom quote only. Our coating systems chemically cross-link into the clear coat at the molecular level, so final pricing depends on vehicle size, paint condition, preparation needed, coating option, work location, weather, and your goals. Ceramic coatings improve gloss, water behavior, protection, and ease of maintenance, but they are not scratch-proof, chip-proof, water-spot-proof, or maintenance-free.

Do you offer paint correction?

Yes — by consultation and custom quote only. Using safe diminishing abrasives and precise dual-action machine polishing, correction can improve gloss, reduce swirls, improve clarity, and enhance the paint's appearance. Final pricing depends on vehicle size, paint condition, and the correction level needed. Paint correction does not fix deep scratches, rock chips, peeling clear coat, thin paint, body damage, rust, or defects beyond what can be safely polished out.

Do you offer touch-up paint?

Yes, by quote, for small paint chips and select minor paint defects. Touch-up paint can improve appearance, but it doesn't make damage invisible — it is not a substitute for body shop repair, repainting, panel refinishing, paint blending, dent repair, or rust repair. A non-refundable custom paint or material deposit may be required before custom paint is ordered.

FAQ

Scheduling, weather & cancellations

What is your cancellation and rescheduling policy?

You may cancel or reschedule online up to 48 hours before your appointment. If that window has closed, please call or text us at (615) 339-9763 to discuss options. Same-day cancellations, no-shows, inaccessible vehicles, unsafe work locations, restricted locations, or failure to provide access may result in loss of your deposit. Weather-related rescheduling is handled separately from customer cancellations.

What happens if weather is bad?

As a mobile service, many of our appointments happen outdoors, and weather can affect safety, product performance, drying time, ceramic coating cure time, paint correction visibility, and final results. We may reschedule for rain, storms, lightning, high wind, extreme heat, freezing temperatures, heavy pollen, dust, unsafe ground conditions, wet work surfaces, or lack of suitable coverage. When we determine weather is unsuitable, we prioritize rescheduling to the next date that works for both of us.

Terms & Conditions

The terms that govern every appointment

By booking, requesting, approving, or receiving service from Zaukus Mobile Detailing, the customer agrees to these Terms of Service.

Booking Review

Booking requests are not automatically confirmed. All appointment requests are reviewed before confirmation. Zaukus Mobile Detailing may review the selected service, vehicle condition, uploaded photos, location, weather, timing, add-ons, and deposit requirements before confirming an appointment.

Zaukus Mobile Detailing may request more information, recommend a different service, adjust the quote, request a deposit, or suggest a different appointment time before confirming service.

Mobile Service Location

Zaukus Mobile Detailing is a mobile service. The customer is responsible for providing a safe, legal, and accessible location for service.

Service may be declined, paused, rescheduled, or cancelled if the location is unsafe, restricted, inaccessible, or unsuitable for mobile detailing.

Apartment complexes, HOA communities, workplaces, gated areas, parking garages, and restricted lots must allow mobile detailing before service can be performed.

Water and Electric

Zaukus Mobile Detailing provides its own water and electric equipment.

If safe water or electric access is available as a backup option, the customer may let Zaukus Mobile Detailing know during booking. Final pricing may reflect water or electric allowance when appropriate.

Vehicle Condition and Pricing

Listed prices are starting prices based on average vehicle condition. Final pricing may increase for excessive pet hair, heavy stains, sticky spills, sand, mud, odor, excessive bugs, tar, sap, water spots, rough paint, heavy contamination, oversized vehicles, lifted vehicles, work vehicles, neglected interiors or exteriors, or unexpected additional labor.

If extra work is needed, Zaukus Mobile Detailing will discuss it before adding charges.

Add-Ons and Extra Work

Some vehicles require more than the base service. Add-ons may be recommended for pet hair, stain treatment, odor treatment, heavy bugs, tar, sap, water spots, iron contamination, clay towel decontamination, ceramic spray sealant, glass coating, or additional correction time.

Base services do not automatically include severe-condition work, paint correction, ceramic coating, touch-up paint, full restoration work, or specialty services unless specifically listed or quoted.

Photos and Documentation

Customers may be asked to upload photos before service so Zaukus Mobile Detailing can review vehicle condition and service needs.

Zaukus Mobile Detailing may take before, during, and after photos for documentation, quality control, estimates, damage documentation, and marketing. If the customer does not want photos used for marketing, the customer should notify Zaukus Mobile Detailing in writing before service begins.

Personal Items

Customers should remove personal items, valuables, documents, electronics, child seats, loose belongings, firearms, medication, cash, and fragile items before service.

Zaukus Mobile Detailing is not responsible for unsecured personal items left inside the vehicle.

Service Expectations

Detailing improves cleanliness, appearance, gloss, protection, and ease of maintenance, but does not guarantee perfect or like-new results.

Permanent stains, deep scratches, rock chips, etching, oxidation, faded trim, worn leather, cracked plastic, damaged clear coat, peeling paint, old odors, prior damage, or heavy wear may remain.

Interior Service Limitations

Interior detailing may improve carpets, seats, plastics, leather, vinyl, glass, and trim. Stain and odor removal are not guaranteed.

Zaukus Mobile Detailing does not currently offer mold remediation or biohazard cleanup. If mold, bodily fluids, hazardous waste, pest infestation, or unsafe contamination is found, service may be declined or stopped.

Exterior Service Limitations

Exterior services may improve cleanliness, gloss, and protection. Base exterior services do not include full paint correction, permanent water spot removal, oxidation removal, deep scratch removal, rock chip repair, collision or structural repairs, repainting, or ceramic coating unless specifically quoted.

Paint Correction

Paint correction services are quote-required.

Paint correction may reduce swirls, haze, oxidation, and certain surface defects, but it does not guarantee complete defect removal.

Paint correction does not fix deep scratches, scratches that catch a fingernail, rock chips, peeling clear coat, thin paint, failing paint, body damage, dents, rust, repaint defects, or defects beyond safe polishing.

Ceramic Coating

Ceramic coating services are quote-required.

Ceramic coatings can improve gloss, water behavior, protection, surface slickness, and ease of maintenance. Ceramic coatings are not scratch-proof, chip-proof, water-spot-proof, maintenance-free, or a replacement for washing or paint protection film.

Ceramic coating results and durability depend on paint preparation, vehicle storage, wash habits, climate, exposure, and customer aftercare.

Touch-Up Paint

Touch-up paint services are quote-required.

Touch-up paint may improve the appearance of small chips or minor defects, but it does not make damage invisible. Touch-up paint is not a substitute for body shop repair, paint blending, panel repainting, dent repair, rust repair, or professional refinishing.

A non-refundable custom paint or material deposit may be required before custom paint is ordered. Once paint or custom materials are ordered, that deposit is non-refundable. If the customer cancels after paint is ordered, or if the full bottle is not used, the customer may keep the remaining paint bottle when applicable.

CARFAX and Vehicle Records

Zaukus Mobile Detailing may collect vehicle information such as license plate, vehicle year, make, model, and related vehicle profile details.

If CARFAX reporting is enabled, certain eligible services or records may be reported through Fieldd/CARFAX or connected systems. The customer is responsible for entering accurate vehicle information.

Deposits

Some appointments may require a deposit after the request is reviewed.

Deposits may be required for standard details, large vehicles, heavily soiled vehicles, paint correction, ceramic coating, touch-up paint, custom material orders, high-demand appointment times, or quote-required services.

Deposits reserve appointment time and are applied toward the final balance unless otherwise stated.

Cancellation and Rescheduling Policy

Customers may cancel or reschedule online up to 48 hours before the appointment.

If the online cancellation or rescheduling window has closed, the customer must call or text Zaukus Mobile Detailing at (615) 339-9763.

Same-day cancellations, no-shows, inaccessible vehicles, locked vehicles, unsafe work locations, restricted service areas, or failure to provide required access may result in loss of deposit.

High-value services, including paint correction and ceramic coating, may have stricter deposit and rescheduling requirements because they reserve larger schedule blocks and require additional preparation.

Custom paint or material deposits are non-refundable once materials are ordered.

Weather Policy

Zaukus Mobile Detailing is a mobile service, and many services are performed outdoors. Weather can affect safety, product performance, drying time, ceramic coating curing, paint correction visibility, and final results.

Zaukus Mobile Detailing may reschedule due to rain, storms, lightning, high wind, extreme heat, freezing temperatures, heavy pollen, dust, unsafe ground conditions, wet work surfaces, lack of suitable coverage, or any condition that may affect service quality or safety.

Weather-related rescheduling is handled differently from customer cancellation. If Zaukus Mobile Detailing determines weather is unsuitable, the appointment will be prioritized for rescheduling to the next available date that works for both the customer and the business.

Arrival Windows and Service Times

Appointment times and service durations are estimates, not guarantees.

Service time may vary based on vehicle size, condition, add-ons, weather, setup needs, location access, customer requests, or unexpected contamination or damage.

Zaukus Mobile Detailing will communicate significant delays when possible.

Maintenance and Subscription Services

Maintenance or subscription-style details may be offered to approved recurring customers only.

Maintenance services are intended to maintain vehicles that are already in good condition. First-time vehicles or heavily soiled vehicles may require a full detail before becoming eligible for maintenance service.

Maintenance eligibility may be denied, paused, or cancelled if the vehicle condition no longer matches maintenance-level service.

  • Following aftercare instructions

Failure to provide accurate information may result in revised pricing, rescheduling, or declined service.

Unpaid balances may delay or prevent future bookings.
