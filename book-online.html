Common Questions

Booking questions, answered.

Do I need to know my exact vehicle size before I book?

No. Fieldd walks you through selecting your vehicle's type and size as part of booking, and pricing adjusts automatically from there.

Is my appointment confirmed the moment I book online?

You'll get an instant text the moment your request comes through. We review vehicle size, condition, service, and location before final confirmation, and we'll reach out first if anything needs adjusting. Booking through Fieldd reserves your appointment time, but we do reserve the right to reschedule or cancel when necessary — we prioritize rescheduling over cancelling and aim to avoid both whenever possible.

Will my detailing show up on my vehicle's CARFAX report?

Zaukus Mobile Detailing uses Fieldd's CARFAX integration to report eligible services to your vehicle's CARFAX service history, giving you documented proof of professional care.
