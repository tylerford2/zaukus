Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a detail take?

Professional details typically take 2-4 hours depending on your vehicle's size and condition. We'll provide a time estimate when you book.

Do you use safe products?

Yes! We use only pH-balanced, paint-safe products and hand-wash methods to protect your vehicle's finish. No harsh chemicals or automated brushes.

How long does ceramic coating last?

Professional ceramic coatings typically last 1-3 years with proper maintenance. We recommend monthly maintenance to maximize longevity.

Can you remove swirl marks?

Yes! Paint correction can remove light to moderate swirls and scratches. Severe damage may require multiple correction passes.

Do you work in winter?

We work year-round! We take extra precautions in cold weather to ensure product application and drying are optimal.

What areas do you service?

We primarily service La Vergne, TN and surrounding Middle Tennessee areas. Contact us to confirm service availability for your location.
