Our Services

Detailing services designed to protect, refine, and preserve your vehicle.

Every appointment is built around precision, craftsmanship, and protection so your vehicle looks exceptional and stays protected long after the service ends.

Zaukus Mobile Detailing is built around a structured process that prioritizes technique over shortcuts. Based in La Vergne, TN, we provide a professional alternative to high-volume, friction-based washing, focusing on the long-term integrity of your vehicle’s surfaces.

Professional Detailing

A proper detail is about more than a clean car; it’s about safe maintenance. We use touch-safe hand-washing methods and pH-balanced decontamination to remove grit and road film without creating the swirl marks or marring often caused by automated brushes.

  • Exterior wash and dry
  • Interior vacuum and wipe-down
  • Glass and tire dressing
  • Engine bay cleaning

Paint Correction

Paint correction is ideal for vehicles that need a deeper level of refinement. We carefully reduce surface imperfections to restore clarity, depth, and a more polished finish.

  • Swirl mark removal
  • Scratch correction
  • Paint enhancement
  • Machine polishing

Ceramic Coating

Ceramic coating provides durable protection with enhanced hydrophobic performance, UV defense, and a glossy finish that makes long-term maintenance easier.

  • Multi-year protection
  • Hydrophobic finish
  • UV protection
  • Easy maintenance

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a detail take?

Professional details typically take 2-4 hours depending on your vehicle's size and condition. We'll provide a time estimate when you book.

Do you use safe products?

Yes! We use only pH-balanced, paint-safe products and hand-wash methods to protect your vehicle's finish. No harsh chemicals or automated brushes.

How long does ceramic coating last?

Professional ceramic coatings typically last 1-3 years with proper maintenance. We recommend monthly maintenance to maximize longevity.

Can you remove swirl marks?

Yes! Paint correction can remove light to moderate swirls and scratches. Severe damage may require multiple correction passes.

Do you work in winter?

We work year-round! We take extra precautions in cold weather to ensure product application and drying are optimal.

What areas do you service?

We primarily service La Vergne, TN and surrounding Middle Tennessee areas. Contact us to confirm service availability for your location.
