Our Services

Detailing services designed to protect, refine, and preserve your vehicle.

Zaukus Mobile Detailing provides professional auto detailing, paint correction, and ceramic coating throughout Middle Tennessee, bringing premium mobile equipment directly to your driveway or office for meticulous, paint-safe care without a shop visit. Every step — from the initial wash to final cure — happens with the same controlled precision you'd expect from a dedicated studio, just parked in your driveway instead.

Professional Mobile Detailing

More than a clean car — it's total mechanical decontamination. Our exterior wash relies on an advanced polymer encapsulation method that physically surrounds and lifts dirt particles away from the paint before they ever have a chance to drag across the surface, eliminating wash-induced marring and scratching without leaving behind soapy residue. Drying is finished with pneumatic air tools from our onboard compressor for a controlled, zero-contact result. Inside the cabin, we deep-clean every high-touch surface and treat upholstery safely, without leaving heavy, oily, dust-attracting residues behind. Packages are organized by vehicle size, from coupes to van and fleet.

  • Interior Detail
  • Exterior Wash & Protection
  • Full Detail
  • Sized for coupe through van/fleet

Precision Paint Correction

A permanent mechanical process — never a temporary mask. Specialized diminishing abrasives paired with precise dual-action machine polishers safely shave down microscopic imperfections and swirls in the clear coat, permanently leveling the surface for true depth and absolute optical reflection. Our long-cycling abrasive emulsions break down evenly over each polishing pass, so trim, plastics, and body lines stay clean of the white dust and dried compound residue that ordinary polishes leave behind.

  • One-Step Enhancement
  • Two-Step Correction
  • Touch-Up Paint

Premium Ceramic Coating Protection

Our coatings chemically cross-link into the clear coat at the molecular level, forming a perfectly flat, glass-like barrier with extreme hydrophobic performance, intense gloss, and protection against UV rays and chemical fallout. Each tier is built on a premium multi-resin blend engineered for a distinct purpose in the finish: a durable base resin bonds first, Silicon Carbide (SiC) reinforces the layer against extreme chemical and etching resistance, and a finishing charge of Nanodiamond and Silicon Nitride particulate delivers the self-cleaning slickness and mirror-like depth the coating is known for.

  • 3-Year Ceramic Protection
  • 6-Year Premium Ceramic Package
  • Advanced Ceramic Coating

Precision Fusing Requires Precision Preparation

True chemical bonding is impossible over embedded road grime, iron contamination, or swirling. Because our professional coating systems require a meticulous multi-step decontamination wash, physical clay bar purification, and intensive machine paint correction to properly flatten and level the clear coat, all coating packages are handled strictly via consultation and custom quoting. Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a detail take?

Professional details typically take 2-4 hours depending on your vehicle's size and condition. We'll provide a time estimate when you book.

Do you use safe products?

Yes! We use only pH-balanced, paint-safe products and hand-wash methods to protect your vehicle's finish, including a polymer encapsulation wash that physically lifts dirt away from the paint rather than dragging it across the surface. No harsh chemicals or automated brushes.

How long does ceramic coating last?

It depends on which coating tier you choose and how you care for your vehicle afterward. Our professional coatings are rated between 3 and 6+ years, and our top-tier package can even last the lifetime of the vehicle with proper care — but that rating is a baseline, not a guarantee. Harsh automatic washes or neglected maintenance can wear down even our best coating quickly. We recommend monthly maintenance to help maximize longevity.

Can you remove swirl marks?

Yes! Paint correction can remove light to moderate swirls and scratches using our long-cycling abrasive emulsions, which level the clear coat without leaving white dust or compound residue in trim and body lines. Severe damage may require multiple correction passes.

Do you work in winter?

We work year-round! We take extra precautions in cold weather to ensure product application and drying are optimal.

What areas do you service?

We primarily service La Vergne, TN and surrounding Middle Tennessee areas. Contact us to confirm service availability for your location.
