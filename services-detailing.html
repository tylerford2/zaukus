Mobile Detailing Packages for La Vergne & Middle Tennessee

Professional detailing from Zaukus combines a pH-neutral, paint-safe exterior wash with total interior decontamination, delivered mobile to your home or office throughout Middle Tennessee. We bring our own water tank, pressure washer, independent generator power, and industrial air compressor with pneumatic tools, so every package runs on the same controlled process whether we're parked in a downtown garage or a rural driveway. Every package is sized to your vehicle, from coupes to full-size vans and fleets.

A proper detail is about more than a clean car — it's total mechanical decontamination. Every wash uses a highly lubricated, pH-neutral, surface-safe decontamination procedure built on advanced polymer encapsulation, which physically surrounds and lifts dirt particles away from the paint before they can drag across the surface, eliminating wash-induced marring and scratching without leaving behind soapy residue. Drying is finished with pneumatic air tools from our onboard compressor for a controlled, zero-contact result. Inside the cabin, we deep-clean every high-touch surface and treat upholstery safely, without leaving behind the heavy, oily, dust-attracting residues common with over-the-counter products.

Every vehicle is different, so we organize our detailing packages by size and layout. Select your vehicle category below to see the three core packages we offer for it.

Interior Detail includes

  • Full interior vacuum
  • Compressed air blowout of cracks, crevices, vents, and trim gaps
  • Dash, console, cupholders, and door panels cleaned
  • Interior trim and plastic surfaces wiped down
  • Floor mats cleaned
  • Door jamb wipe-down
  • Interior glass cleaned
  • Light deodorizer

Exterior Wash & Protection includes

  • Exterior hand wash
  • Wheels, tires, and wheel wells cleaned
  • Tire dressing applied
  • Exterior windows and mirrors cleaned
  • Door jamb wipe-down
  • Light bug and road grime cleanup
  • Spray protection applied for added gloss

Full Detail includes

  • Every step from Interior Detail above
  • Every step from Exterior Wash & Protection above
  • Completed together in one mobile visit
